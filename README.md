# Gravix Layer feedback

This repository is the place to share input on **Gravix Layer**—bugs, feature ideas, rough edges in the product or docs, and anything else that would make the experience better. Please open a **GitHub issue** and include as much context as you can: what you tried, what you expected, what happened instead, and any links or screenshots that might help.

We aim to acknowledge new issues within **one business day**. (Exact timelines can vary with volume and complexity.)

**General product or questions:** [support@gravixlayer.ai](mailto:support@gravixlayer.ai)
